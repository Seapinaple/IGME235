Description

a game of galactic conquest in which three to eight players take on the role of one of twenty five factions vying for galactic domination through military might, political maneuvering, and economic bargaining.

Game Categories

  • Genre: Stragety, Political, Wargame
  • Players: 3-8
  • Play Time: 4-6 Hours

Publishing Info

  • Designers: Dane Beltrami, Corey Konieczka, Christian T. Petersen
  • Artist: Scott Schomburg
  • Publisher: Fantasy Flight Games
  • Developers: Dane Beltrami and Jason Walden
